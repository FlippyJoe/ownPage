Power BI report- British Airways

One of my first Power BI analysis, performed for practice purposes.
The aim of the analsis is to show the most preferred destination countries by day of the week and time of the day. Utilizing techniques such as drill down function, tooltips, the viewer can get detailed information of the travellers`s behaviour by departure and destination country thoughout the year.

MS Excel report- Inventory Management

Working in retail sales inspired me to create a sheet that helps to predict the expectable quantity of expiring products and plan the future orders accordingly. The calculations include economic order quantity, minimum order quantity and average daily sales of the products. To succesfully apply ABC analysis the products are broken down into categories and sub-categories.
